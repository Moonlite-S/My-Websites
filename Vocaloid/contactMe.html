Q - The pictures are a little bit jumpy...

A - You're using Internet Explorer, aren't you? First off, don't look at it too much, as it might make your head dizzy.
Second, in your options, there should be a tab called 'Advanced' and a grouping named 'Browsing'. Then, at the bottom of the group, de-select 'Use Smooth Scrolling'. You should then restart your browser to make sure. That should fix your problem.

Q - The "Hide/Show" button doesn't hide/show!

A - Althought I haven't seen this happen yet, the Hide/Show button is a work-in-progress, so unexpected things may occur.
That being said, I am planning more functions for it so debugging and fixing the button is last on my list.
