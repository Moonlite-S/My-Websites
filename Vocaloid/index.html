So What Is Vocaloid?

Vocaloid is a singing voice synthesizer computer software developed by Yamaha Co. around the beginning of January, 2004. Each Vocaloid is made by an actual person's voice, so people can make music without having to contact the actual person. That said, every Vocaloid usually has some sort of personality to go with them, depending on how they were designed.
